Glossary

TermDefinition
PIProgram Increment — a planning period (typically 8–12 weeks)
SPStory Points — a relative measure of effort
VelocityStory points completed per sprint
ThroughputNumber of items completed per time period
Cycle TimeTime from work started to work completed
WIPWork In Progress — items currently being worked on
CFDCumulative Flow Diagram — shows work item flow over time
SpilloverWork not completed in a sprint, carried to the next
DisruptionUnplanned work pulled into a sprint
Monte CarloProbabilistic simulation using historical data to forecast outcomes
